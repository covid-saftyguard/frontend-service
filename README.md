# COVID SafeGuard

The novel COVID-19 virus been in our lives for the past year and half. Globally, this virus has changed the way we 
all interact in public settings. However, now that there are vaccines soon to be released to the public, we as a society can 
now take the precautions necessary to protect our loved ones. COVID SafeGuard is the perfect app you and your family needs in these
uncertain times; from finding and navigating vaccine locations that have the vaccines in stock, to reading up on how to 
protect and maintain healthy behaviors, COVID SafeGuard will keep you rest assured with our mobile app. 

## Installation 
  COVID SafeGuard operates on React Native. Please make sure that you have Expo installed. 
    ```npm install --global expo-cli
    ```
  You can also download the expo app on the app store to see COVID SafeGuard on your mobile device. 
  
  ## Usage 
    In the terminal, you can run
      ```expo start```
   once you have expo installed. Once you run expo start, a window will open up with a QR code. From here, you can scan the QR code with your camera app to see COVID SafeGuard on your phone, 
   or if you have and android simulator you can use that as well.
